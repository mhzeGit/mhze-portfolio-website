About Me

MH

Game Developer & 3D Artist

My journey in game development began in 2021 and has encompassed creating several published horror games and a diverse portfolio of 3D assets. Over the past four years, I've developed proficiency in multiple game engines, programming languages, and a comprehensive skillset within Blender.

What started as freelance animation work evolved into my passion for game development. I've successfully released notable games that have gained popularity on itch.io, showcasing my dedication and growth within this exciting industry.

I specialize in creating immersive gaming experiences from concept to completion, combining technical programming skills with artistic 3D modeling and animation expertise.
