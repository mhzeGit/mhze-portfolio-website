Animation Process & Methodology

My animation workflow has evolved through experimentation and study of traditional animation principles. Each project begins with thorough planning, reference gathering, and rough blocking before moving into detailed animation and polishing phases.

1. Concept & Reference

Every animation starts with gathering reference material, studying real-world movement, and planning the key storytelling moments.

2. Blocking & Timing

Rough keyframes establish the main poses and timing, focusing on clear silhouettes and strong poses that communicate the action.

3. Spacing & Breakdown

Adding breakdown frames and adjusting spacing to create convincing weight and realistic motion timing.

4. Polish & Secondary

Final pass adding secondary animation, overlapping action, and subtle details that bring the animation to life.
